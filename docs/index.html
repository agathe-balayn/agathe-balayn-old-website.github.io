PhD student in AI and Ethics, Delft University of Technology

My work focuses on applications of Artificial Intelligence which have an impact on society.
I initially investigated Machine and Deep Learning to facilitate the interaction between human operators and robotic arms, and to enable better communication between hearing and deaf people.
Now, I direct my projects on the different ethical implications that the use of Machine Learning methods have in various types of systems. I mainly look at unfairness and privacy-infringement issues.

I am a PhD candidate at the Web Information Systems (WIS) Group in the Faculty of Electrical Engineering, Mathematics and Computer Science (EEMCS/EWI) of the Delft University of Technology. My promoter is Dr. Alessandro Bozzon and my co-promoters are Dr. Christoph Lofi and Dr. Asterios Katsifodimos.
